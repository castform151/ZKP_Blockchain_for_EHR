What all can be stored in block?

1)Basic patient information such as name, date of birth, gender, contact details, and social security number
2)Past and current medical conditions, diagnoses, treatments, surgeries, hospitalizations, and medication history
3)Allergies and sensitivities
4)Immunization records (Covid Vaccine)
5)Laboratory results: Results of laboratory tests, such as blood tests, urine tests, radiology reports, and pathology reports.
6)Prescription data: Information about prescribed medications, including the medication name, dosage, frequency, and duration of use.
7)Consent and authorization records: Documentation of patient consent and authorizations for specific treatments or sharing of health information.
8)Health insurance information
